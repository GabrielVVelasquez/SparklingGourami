Temperament & Tank Mates

Because of their relatively small size and skittish nature, avoid keeping Sparkling Gouramis with any aggressive or boisterous/fast swimming fish such as Black Skirt Tetras and Tiger Barbs, as they'll nip at your gouramis' fins and cause them stress. Good tankmates for Sparkling Gouramis would be peaceful, similarly-sized fish and invertebrates.
