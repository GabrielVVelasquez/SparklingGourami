Diet & Feeding

In the wild, Sparkling Gouramis typically prey on small insects, larvae, invertebrates, and worms that live in the water, as well as those unlucky uneough to fall on its surface. However in the home aquarium, these fish will accept a wide range of live, frozen, and dried foods. Although some may eat flakes and floating pellets, these fish prefer to eat sinking foods as these fish are primarily middle to bottom dwellers.

As with any other fish, ensure to offer a variety of foods. Supplementing with live or frozen foods would be ideal, as doig so can enhance the colors of your fish and help condition them to breed.

Here are some foods that I recommend (I have no affiliation with any of these brands):


Ultra Fresh Tropical Micro Pellets


Fluval Bug Bites Tropical Granules


Hikari Freeze Dried Spirulina Brine Shrimp


Hikari Freeze Dried Tubifex Worms


Super Cichlids Freeze Dried Blackworms

(I don't recommend flake foods because they have a short shelf life, they have a tendency to dissolve relatively fast once they're put in water, and they aren't as nutrionally dense compared to pellets or granules)
